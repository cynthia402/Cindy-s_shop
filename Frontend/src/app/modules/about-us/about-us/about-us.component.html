About Us



Welcome to Cindy's Shop, your premier destination for authentic Kiondo bags and Maasai shoes, proudly located in the vibrant city of Nairobi, Kenya. Although our roots are firmly planted in this bustling metropolis, we primarily operate online to reach a global audience, ensuring everyone has access to our unique and handcrafted products.


At Cindy's Shop, we are passionate about preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Kenya. Our Kiondo bags are meticulously woven by skilled artisans, each piece telling a story of tradition and craftsmanship. These bags are not just accessories; they are a testament to the enduring beauty of Kenyan culture.


In addition to our exquisite Kiondo bags, we offer a stunning collection of Maasai shoes. These shoes are handmade with intricate beadwork and vibrant colors that reflect the spirit and history of the Maasai people. Each pair is a work of art, combining comfort and style with a touch of heritage.


Our mission at Cindy's Shop is to provide high-quality, culturally significant products while supporting local artisans and communities. By choosing our products, you are not only getting beautiful and unique items but also contributing to the livelihoods of the talented craftsmen and women of Kenya.


We invite you to explore our online store and discover the beauty and authenticity of our Kiondo bags and Maasai shoes. Whether you are looking for a special gift or a unique addition to your wardrobe, Cindy's Shop offers something truly special. Thank you for supporting our journey and celebrating the rich traditions of Kenya with us.
